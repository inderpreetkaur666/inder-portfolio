Cadillac — Integrated Launch & Communications

Automotive • Launch & Comms • Dealer Enablement

Situation

Cadillac needed consistent workflows and collateral to support dealer executions and EV launches, while maintaining speed and brand standards.

My Role

Built creative briefs, asset workflows, and enablement kits. Coordinated agencies, dealers, and internal teams to streamline approvals and delivery.

Approach

  • Standardized intake → brief → approvals → distribution.
  • Central launch tracker with statuses and dependencies.
  • Reusable templates and localization process.

Results

  • Shorter turnaround (fewer revision cycles).
  • Cleaner dealer handoffs and fewer escalations.
  • EV launch communications delivered on time and on brand.
