Cadillac — Integrated Launch & Communications

Automotive Launch & Comms Dealer Enablement

Coordinated communications and enablement for dealer programs and EV launches, while maintaining speed and brand standards across markets.

Snapshot

Standardized intake → briefs → approvals → distribution. Central tracker, reusable templates, and localized assets enabled faster turnarounds and cleaner handoffs.

Situation

Cadillac needed consistent workflows and collateral to support dealer execution and EV launches at scale.

My Role

Built creative briefs, asset workflows, and enablement kits. Coordinated agencies, dealers, and internal teams to streamline approvals and delivery.

Approach

  • Standardized intake & approval chain with clear RACI.
  • Central launch tracker with statuses, owners, and dependencies.
  • Reusable templates and localization process.
