Old Navy — Seasonal Retail Programs

RetailProgramsToolkits

Built reusable toolkits and a promo calendar to align merchandising, creative, and stores for timely seasonal execution.

Snapshot

Promo calendar + toolkit templates → fewer ad-hoc requests, faster approvals, clearer store comms.

Objective

Plan and roll out seasonal assortments and promotions across stores, on time and with consistent creative.

My Role

Coordinated with merchandising, creative, store ops; maintained calendar and deliverables.

Approach

  • Toolkit system (posters, price cards, FAQs).
  • Central promo calendar aligned to delivery windows.
  • Clear approval path for store communications.
