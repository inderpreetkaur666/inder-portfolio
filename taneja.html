Taneja — Events & Internal Communications

Internal Comms • Newsletters • Invites • Templates

Situation

Teams needed clearer, on-brand event comms and a repeatable system for invites, agendas, and post-event updates.

My Role

Built a small design system for emails and internal docs, set an editorial calendar, and created reusable assets.

Approach

  • Modular templates for invites, reminders, and recaps.
  • Editorial calendar with owners and approvals.
  • Distribution playbook (channels, timing, tone).

Results

  • Faster comms with fewer ad-hoc requests.
  • Higher RSVP and attendance consistency.
